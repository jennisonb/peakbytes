ChemicalPeel Pre-Treatment Instructions

Pregnancy or breastfeeding

Any recent facial surgical procedures

Herpes Simplex/ Prone to cold sores

Keloid scarring

Thyroid Conditions

Hyperpigmentation tendencies

If you have dark skin

Use of Accutane in the last 24 months

Use of steroids like prednisone

If you are HIV positive or have AIDS

Diabetes or any auto immune disease

Radiation or chemo therapy in the last 12 months

Microdermabrasion/other peels within the last 2 weeks

One to two weeks prior to treatment, the following treatments and products should be avoided:

Waxing, depilatories, electrolysis, or laser hair removal on treated area for two weeks

 No sun exposure or tanning for one week

Laser skin rejuvenation on treated area for two weeks

Botox® & Dermal Filler Injections on or around the treated area for two weeks

Tretinoin or any retinoic acid derivatives (Renova, Refissa, Retinol, Atralin, Avita, Tazorac or Avage, Ziana), Azelex, or Differin for two weeks
